CUP Validation Rules
Required Fields
  • ISBN, Trim Height, Trim Width, Extent, Paper, Colour, Quality, Binding Style
Valid Values
  • Binding: Cased, Limp
  • Paper: CUP MunkenPure 80 gsm, Navigator 80 gsm, Clairjet 90 gsm, Magno Matt 90 gsm
  • Colour: Mono, Colour
  • Quality/Route: Standard, Premium
  • Trim Sizes: 140x216, 152x229, 156x234, 170x244, 189x246, 178x254, 203x254, 216x280
Paper Compatibility Rules
  • CUP MunkenPure 80 gsm: Must be Mono + Standard
  • Navigator 80 gsm: Must be Mono + Standard
  • Clairjet 90 gsm: Must be Colour + Standard
  • Magno Matt 90 gsm: Must be Premium (Mono or Colour allowed)
Extent Rounding
  • Width ≤156mm: Extent must be divisible by 6
  • Width ≥157mm: Extent must be divisible by 4
